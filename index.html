FILM SOUND


I am a Helsinki based sound designer with a Master's degree on film sound design from Aalto University.
I have a decade of experience on all kinds of projects ranging from ads to feature length feature films and working in pre- to postproduction.
I am a founding member of the reality-tv and foley juggernaut Fortress Soundworks, which I left to pursue the more artistic side of films with my studies.
